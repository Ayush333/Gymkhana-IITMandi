Music

Club

Vision

Music Club, IIT Mandi aims at bringing up the best talent within the students by providing them a healthy atmosphere, provided by a vast collection of instrument in the club in assisted by the community of the Music Club.

Objective

1. Organise the musical family within the institution together and bring out the best of performances.
2. Use the diverse talent within the institute and make others learn and expand our reach.
3. Keep a good track of maintenance of the musical resources available to the club and expand the resources in the future.

Goal

To upbring the musical talent amongst the student through the whole institute and make students learn and grow up a healthy culture toward their interests in music.
