Programming

Club

Vision

The vision of the Club is to increase the general programming aptitude of each and every student in IIT Mandi and make people realise the power of computers and programming.

Objective

Overall development in all fields of programming including competitive programming, web development and mobile app development. Conduct workshops on these fields and promote students to try and progress.

Goal

To increase the general aptitude of programming and to create real-world applications which can compete in the world market.
