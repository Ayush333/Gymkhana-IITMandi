About us

Art Geeks is the official art club of IIT Mandi.
We at Art Geeks feel that a college immersed in technology and science requires an equal dose of art to remind everyone of the beautiful environment around us.
The club provides a platform to all art and craft enthusiasts of the college to learn, develop and exhibit their skills.
This is the club to be for all the art lovers, enthusiasts and critics residing in the campus.
